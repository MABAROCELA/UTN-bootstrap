NINA
EAU DE TOILETTE

Inspired by nature, Nina by Nina Ricci is a clean and vegan fragrance as delightfully fresh and tantalizing as a toffee apple. It's a modern citrus-floral scent, bursting with positivity and a touch of playfulness. For an eco-responsible approach, Nina's iconic apple-shaped bottle is available in an 80ml refillable format.

NINA NATURE
LIMITED EDITION

Reconnect to nature with the new clean and vegan limited edition Nina Nature Eau de Toilette by Nina Ricci. As crisp as a just-picked apple glistening with dew in the clear morning light. Blending natural apple extract with jasmine and vanilla, this green floral scent immerses you in fresh and flourishing nature wherever you are.

NINA ROSE
EAU DE PARFUM

The power of rose. In the wake of Nina and Nina Rouge, Nina Rose is an infusion of optimism and joie de vivre. It is a fragrance that expresses impertinent and vibrant femininity.
